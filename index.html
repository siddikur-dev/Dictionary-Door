Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes — many of our basic lessons and resources are free. We also offer optional premium courses for advanced study.

3. Do I need to create an account?

Creating an account saves your progress and unlocks certain features, but you can explore content without signing up.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

Use flashcards, read graded stories, practice with quizzes, and try using new words in sentences every day.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes — certificates are available for some paid courses after you pass the final assessments.
